About me

Johan Hentze Svenden is an engineer with a passion for coding, dynamics, machine learning and optimization. He has previous experience working in Scuderia Ferrari, as a Performance Methodology Engineer and before this has worked with Massive Motorsport as a Data Engineer. He has a bachelor’s in automotive engineering from Brighton University, to which he builds upon with a Master in Motorsport Engineering from Oxford Brookes University. Throughout these he has also had Leadership roles inside of the university’s Formula Student teams, in which the Oxford Brookes Racing team achieved top UK team and 2nd place overall at the FSUK competition.
